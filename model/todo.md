## Instructions for Upload
1. Log in as Super Admin: `admin@vdeskconnect.com` / `SuperAdmin@2026!`
2. Go to `/admin/illustrations`
3. Click "Upload New Pack"
4. Enter pack name: `vdesk-v1`
5. Select all SVG files from `model/illustrations/`
6. Upload → then click "Activate" to make them live across the app
